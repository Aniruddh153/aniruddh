Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel during your free trial period, you won't be charged. If you cancel after the trial period, you'll still have access to premium features until the end of your billing cycle.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new features will be available immediately, and you'll be charged the prorated difference. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a limit to how much content I can download?

The Basic plan allows up to 50 downloads per month. The Pro and Ultimate plans offer unlimited downloads. All downloaded content is for personal use only and should not be redistributed.

How often is new premium content added?

We add new premium content weekly, including exclusive questions, topic guides, and previous year papers. We also update our content based on the latest exam patterns and syllabus changes.

Can I share my premium account with others?

No, premium accounts are for individual use only. Our systems can detect multiple simultaneous logins from different locations, which may result in account suspension. If you need accounts for multiple users, please contact us about our institutional plans.

Are the certificates recognized by institutions?

Our certificates demonstrate your knowledge and dedication to learning. While they are not accredited by educational institutions, many of our users have reported that they add value to their resumes and demonstrate their commitment to continuous learning.
